Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to PlayGame.click. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website. We are committed to ensuring the privacy and security of our visitors while providing an enjoyable gaming experience.

Information We Collect

Automatically Collected Information:

  • Log data (IP address, browser type, access times)
  • Device information (operating system, screen resolution)
  • Usage data (pages visited, games played)

Local Storage Data:

  • Recently played games history
  • Game preferences and settings

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our gaming service
  • To improve website performance and user experience
  • To analyze usage patterns and optimize game offerings
  • To detect and prevent technical issues

Embedded Games and Third-Party Content

Our website features games that are embedded via iframes from various gaming platforms. Please note:

  • These games may have their own privacy policies and data collection practices
  • We do not control or have access to any data collected by embedded games
  • We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of individual game providers

Cookies and Local Storage

We use local storage to enhance your gaming experience:

  • To remember your recently played games
  • To maintain game preferences and settings
  • To improve website performance

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. However, please note that no method of internet transmission is 100% secure.

Children's Privacy

Our website is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us to have it removed.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
