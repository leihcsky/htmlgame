Cookie Policy

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better gaming experience by remembering your preferences and usage patterns.

How We Use Cookies

Essential Cookies:

  • To maintain basic website functionality
  • To remember your game preferences
  • To keep track of your recently played games

Performance Cookies:

  • To analyze how visitors use our website
  • To identify and fix technical issues
  • To improve website performance

Third-Party Cookies

Our website includes embedded games from third-party providers. These games may use their own cookies or similar technologies. We recommend reviewing the cookie policies of individual game providers.

Local Storage

We use browser's local storage to:

  • Save your recently played games list
  • Remember your game preferences
  • Improve loading times of frequently accessed content

Managing Cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. You can delete all cookies that are already on your computer and you can set most browsers to prevent them from being placed. However, if you do this, you may have to manually adjust some preferences every time you visit our site and some features may not work as intended.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
